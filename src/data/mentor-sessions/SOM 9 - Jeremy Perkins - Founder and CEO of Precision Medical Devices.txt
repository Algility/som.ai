SOM 9: Jeremy Perkins
Founder and CEO of Precision Medical Devices
Recorded July 31st, 2024. Duration 1:17:34.

Jeremy Perkins, Founder and CEO of Precision Medical Devices, breaks down his 4 Pillars to Scaling a Company. He discusses strategies for scaling a small startup into a multi-million dollar company, the role of technical founders in medical device companies, and how to build confidence in product validation. He also covers borrowing money for business, product selection and improvement for patents, entering the medical device industry, and finding the right mentor.

In this mentor session with Jeremy Perkins, the following topics were covered:
  - 4 Pillars to Scaling a Company
  - Technical founders in medical device companies
  - Scaling from startup to multi-million dollar company
  - Building confidence in product validation
  - Transitioning to entrepreneurship
  - Borrowing money for business
  - Product selection and improvement for patents
  - Increasing sales and getting more customers
  - Entering the medical device industry
  - Getting feedback on programs
  - Maintaining a competitive edge with patents
  - Lean startups and finding the right mentor
