SOM 3: Zain Jan
Founder and CEO of Better Earth
Recorded June 19th, 2024. Duration 1:12:02.

Zain Jan, Founder and CEO of Better Earth, shares his story of growing up in poverty to building a 9-figure company by age 27. He provides a masterclass on transforming business and life for efficiency and growth. Key topics include developing systems and delegating to scale from 7 to 9 figures, choosing the right business partners, sourcing leads and building online trust, and the importance of implementing systems early. He also discusses personal models in business and the power of sales.

In this mentor session with Zain Jan, the following topics were covered:
  - Scaling 7-9 figures
  - systems and delegation
  - business partnerships
  - lead sourcing
  - online trust building
  - early systems implementation
  - personal models
  - sales power
