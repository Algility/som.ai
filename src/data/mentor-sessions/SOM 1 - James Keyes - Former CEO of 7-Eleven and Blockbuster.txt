SOM 1: James Keyes
Former CEO of 7-Eleven and Blockbuster
Recorded June 6th, 2024. Duration 1:29:49.

James Keyes, former CEO of 7-Eleven and Blockbuster, shares insights on what it takes to become an amazing CEO. He discusses the importance of prioritizing business focus in today's world, the connection between fear and knowledge, and how to identify red flags in hiring and networking. He also covers the value of an MBA, the importance of building systems early on, and how to transfer skills between different industries. Finally, he touches on his collaboration with Mr. Beast and the importance of building a legacy.
