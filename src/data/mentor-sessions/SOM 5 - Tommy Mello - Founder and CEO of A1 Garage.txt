SOM 5: Tommy Mello
Founder and CEO of A1 Garage
Recorded July 3rd, 2024. Duration 1:15:40.

Tommy Mello, Founder and CEO of A1 Garage, shares his expertise on delegating, creating business systems, and using money efficiently as a tool for growth. He discusses strategies for maintaining growth in a slow economy, overcoming adversity, and marketing for scale. He also covers using software for hiring and qualifying people, the debate between focusing on investors versus building a great business, and the importance of self-talk and mindset in achieving success.
