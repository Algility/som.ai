SOM 2: Edwin Arroyave
Founder and CEO of Skyline Security Management
Recorded June 12th, 2024. Duration 2:06:53.

Edwin Arroyave, Founder and CEO of Skyline Security Management, shares his journey from a rough upbringing in Colombia to becoming a 9-figure entrepreneur. He discusses how faith shaped his life and business, strategies for scaling from 6 to 9 figures, and how to maintain work-life balance. He also covers finding and retaining talent, the importance of routines for discipline, transitioning from employee to entrepreneur, and dealing with imposter syndrome and the fear of failing.

In this mentor session with Edwin Arroyave, the following topics were covered:
  - Faith in business
  - Scaling from 6 to 9 figures
  - Work-life balance
  - Finding and retaining talent
  - Discipline and consistency routines
  - Transitioning from employee to entrepreneur
  - Starting in a new industry
  - Ensuring vision execution
  - Avoiding shiny object syndrome
  - Scaling too fast
  - Imposter syndrome
