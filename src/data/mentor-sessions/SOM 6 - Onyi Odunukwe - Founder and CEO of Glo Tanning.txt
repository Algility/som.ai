SOM 6: Onyi Odunukwe
Founder and CEO of Glo Tanning
Recorded July 10th, 2024. Duration 1:29:23.

Onyi Odunukwe, Founder and CEO of Glo Tanning, discusses his experience building an 8-figure national franchise with over 100 locations. He shares insights on overcoming brand building challenges, effective delegation to a growing team, and the pros and cons of raising capital through banks versus investors. He also covers strategies for diversification and vertical integration, building a company to sell for a high multiple, and how to handle burnout and avoid overwhelm while scaling.

In this mentor session with Onyi Odunukwe, the following topics were covered:
  - Overcoming brand building challenges
  - Starting a franchise business
  - Effective delegation to a growing team
  - Cultural influence on entrepreneurship
  - Raising capital (banks vs. investors)
  - Securing initial financing
  - Strategies for diversification and vertical integration
  - Building a company to sell for a high multiple
  - Handling burnout and avoiding overwhelm
  - Scaling from 1 to 100 locations
