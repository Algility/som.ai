SOM 4: Shawn Meaike
Founder and CEO of Family First Life
Recorded June 26th, 2024. Duration 1:50:25.

Shawn Meaike, Founder and CEO of Family First Life, discusses his journey from an insurance agent to building an almost billion-dollar company. He shares insights on raising kids with grit despite having wealth, deciding whether to sell a business or keep it, and capital allocation strategies. He also covers scaling from 7 to 9 figures after bankruptcy, prioritizing high-leverage activities, building a leadership team, and the truths about wealth that struggling people need to understand.

In this mentor session with Shawn Meaike, the following topics were covered:
  - Raising kids with grit
  - Selling a business vs. keeping it
  - Capital allocation (office vs. real estate)
  - Scaling from 7 to 9 figures after bankruptcy
  - Prioritizing high-leverage activities
  - Building a firm vs. solopreneurship
  - Starting over in a foreign country
  - Truths about wealth
  - Building a leadership team
  - Getting real estate investors
  - Pivoting and backup plans
